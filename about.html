About RPE Lab

RPE Lab exists for one reason: autoregulation is the most useful training concept most lifters never properly implement. The research is solid, the coaches swear by it — but the practical, day-to-day "here's exactly what to do" layer is scattered across podcasts, forum threads, and paywalled programs. We're building that layer.

Everything here follows the same standard: it has to be usable in your next session. Guides give exact protocols, not theory summaries. Tools do the math for you. Reviews are based on published specs, real user consensus, and whether the thing actually serves RPE-based training — we tell you who each product is for and who should skip it.
